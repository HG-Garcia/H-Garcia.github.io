ABOUT US


Started in November of 2021 by two self-driven beauty loving owners, Serenity balms strives to offer a good range of products by focusing on natural ingredients and quality performance. Each product is handmade, crafted with attention to detail, and cruelty free. Coconuts, our main selected ingredient have a natural essense that pairs flawlessly with our lip balms.

We aim to offer a fun new perspective in self care while maintaining stellar customer satisfaction. Use our "contact" page for products suggestions, comments, and/or questions.
